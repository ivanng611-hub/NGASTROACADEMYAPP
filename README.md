PRIVACY POLICY FOR NG ASTRO ACADEMY APP
================================
Last Updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NG Astro Academy APP ("the App", "we", "us") handles
information when you use our mobile application.

────────────────────────────────────────
1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
────────────────────────────────────────

NG Astro Academy APP does NOT collect, transmit, store, or share any personal
information. Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics data.
  • We do NOT use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do NOT send any data to external servers or the internet.

────────────────────────────────────────
2. DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE
────────────────────────────────────────

The App stores the following data LOCALLY on your device only, using the
device's built-in storage (localStorage / WebView storage):

  • Your selected display language (English, Traditional Chinese, or
    Simplified Chinese).
  • Your quiz session history (scores, dates, categories).
  • Your learning progress (viewed constellations, achievement badges,
    total quiz scores).

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, synced, or shared
with anyone. You can delete all locally stored data at any time using the
"Reset Progress" button in the app's Progress tab.

────────────────────────────────────────
3. LOCATION / GPS PERMISSION
────────────────────────────────────────

The App requests optional access to your device's GPS location. This
permission is used solely to auto-fill your latitude and longitude in the
Sky Simulator feature, so the app can calculate accurate star positions for
your location.

  • Location data is used only for local, on-device astronomical calculations.
  • Location data is NEVER transmitted, stored beyond the current session,
    or shared with any third party.
  • Granting location permission is entirely optional. You may manually
    enter any coordinates instead.

────────────────────────────────────────
4. INTERNET PERMISSION
────────────────────────────────────────

The App declares an internet permission solely to support the Progressive
Web App (PWA) service worker for offline caching of app assets. The App
does not make any network requests to external servers during normal use.
All star catalogs, constellation data, quiz questions, and astronomical
calculations are bundled within the App and work entirely offline.

────────────────────────────────────────
5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
────────────────────────────────────────

NG ASTRO ACADEMY APP is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because
we do not collect any personal information from any user, the App complies
with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar
regulations worldwide.

────────────────────────────────────────
6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
────────────────────────────────────────

NG ASTRO ACADEMY APP does not integrate any third-party services, including but not
limited to:

  • No advertising networks (AdMob, etc.)
  • No analytics platforms (Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No social media SDKs (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services

────────────────────────────────────────
7. DATA SECURITY
────────────────────────────────────────

Since all data remains on your device and no data is transmitted over the
internet, the security of your information is governed by your device's own
security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

────────────────────────────────────────
8. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
────────────────────────────────────────

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be
reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and will
be published on the same page where this policy is hosted. Continued use of
the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
